# 🌋 Molten Core (MC) Raid Guide

Welcome to the heart of Blackrock Mountain. This 40-man raid is the first major step into endgame content. Preparedness is 90% of the battle here—bring your **Fire Resistance (FR)** gear and plenty of consumables.

---

## 📋 Pre-Raid Checklist
- [ ] **Attunement:** Complete the "Attunement to the Core" quest (jump through the window in BRD).
- [ ] **Consumables:** Greater Fire Protection Potions (GFPP), Mongoose/Power Elixirs, and Mana Potions.
- [ ] **Fire Resistance:** Tanks should aim for $300+$ FR; Melee/Healers should have $100+$.
- [ ] **Douses:** Ensure at least 8 players have **Quintessence** (from Hydraxian Waterlords) to douse the runes.

---

## ⚔️ Boss Encounters

### 1. Lucifron
The "Curse Boss." The fight is won or lost by your Decursers.
* **The Adds:** Kill the two Flamewaker Protectors first.
* **Mechanic:** *Lucifron's Curse* increases mana costs by 100%. **Mages/Druids must priority decurse.**
* **Impending Doom:** Deals 2,000 Shadow damage after 10 seconds. Priests/Paladins must dispel Magic.

### 2. Magmadar
The giant core hound.
* **Fear:** Every 30 seconds, he uses *Panic*. Warriors must **Stance Dance** to Berserker Rage; Shamans/Paladins use Tremor Totem/Fear Ward.
* **Tranquilizing Shot:** Hunters **must** rotate Tranq Shot to soothe his *Frenzy*.
* **Fire Spit:** Don't stand in the lava patches on the floor.

### 3. Gehennas
Similar to Lucifron but with a healing debuff.
* **Gehennas' Curse:** Reduces healing received by 75%. **Decurse this instantly.**
* **Rain of Fire:** Move out immediately. It hits harder than the standard warlock spell.

### 4. Garr
The "Add Management" fight.
* **Firesworn Adds:** Warlocks must **Banish** as many as possible (usually 4-5).
* **Separation:** Tanks pull the un-banished adds away from Garr. When an add dies, it knocks everyone back.
* **Magma Shackles:** Massive slow. Dispel magic from your casters.

### 5. Baron Geddon
The "Living Bomb" and the source of the *Binding of the Windseeker*.
* **Living Bomb:** If you get this, **RUN** to the designated empty area. You will explode and fly into the air. (Priests: Shield the bomb target so they don't die from fall damage).
* **Ignite Mana:** Drains mana over time. Priests/Paladins must dispel.
* **Inferno:** Geddon stays still and pulses massive AoE fire damage. **Melee: Run out!**

---

### 🔥 The Big Bad: Ragnaros
The Firelord himself. You have **10 minutes** to kill him before he submerges.

| Phase | Mechanic | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **P1: The Fight** | *Wrath of Ragnaros* | Knocks back melee. High FR prevents the "knock-up" damage. |
| | *Hand of Ragnaros* | Knocks back everyone near him. Stay spread out to avoid chain-knocks. |
| | *Magma Splash* | Triggered if no one is in melee range. A tank **must** always be hitting him. |
| **P2: Submerge** | *Sons of Flame* | 8 adds spawn. Warlocks Banish, Mages AoE Frost Nova. Kill them fast! |

> [!WARNING]
> **Ranged & Healers:** Do not stand near the lava edges. If you get knocked into the lava, you lose precious DPS/Healing time and will likely die to fire ticks.

### 💎 Notable Loot
* **Legendaries:** *Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker* (Garr/Geddon) and *Sulfuras, Hand of Ragnaros* (Ragnaros).
* **Tier 1 Sets:** Every boss drops specific pieces for your class "Giantstalker," "Cenarion," "Felheart," etc.
