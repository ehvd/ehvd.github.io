# 👁️ Temple of Ahn'Qiraj (AQ40) Raid Guide

The ancient subterranean prison of the Old God C'Thun. This raid is a massive step up from BWL, requiring high **Nature Resistance (NR)**, precise positioning, and heavy consumable usage (Greater Nature Protection Potions).

---

## 📋 Pre-Raid Checklist
- [ ] **Nature Resistance Gear:** Essential for Princess Huhuran and Viscidus. Tanks: $300+$, Melee: $150-225+$.
- [ ] **Consumables:** Greater Nature Protection Potions (GNPP), Free Action Potions (FAP), and Frost Oil (for Viscidus).
- [ ] **Reputation:** Farm "Brood of Nozdormu" to unlock Tier 2.5 gear quests.
- [ ] **Mechanics:** Ensure everyone has a way to track "Threat" (like TinyThreat) as many bosses here drop or reset aggro.

---

## ⚔️ Boss Encounters

### 1. The Prophet Skeram
The "Split" Boss. Coordination is key to avoid wiping on Arcane Explosions.
* **The Splits:** At 75%, 50%, and 25%, he creates two illusions. Illusions take more damage but hit just as hard. 
* **Strategy:** Assign tanks to the left, right, and center platforms before the pull. Kill illusions first.
* **Mind Control:** He will MC players, increasing their size/damage. Polymorph or CC them immediately; do not kill your teammates.

### 2. Silithid Royalty (The Bug Trio)
Optional. An encounter with three bosses at once. Loot depends on the order they die.
* **The Order:** Usually **Vem -> Yauj -> Kri** (for easiest kill) or **Kri -> Yauj -> Vem** (for best loot).
* **Strategy:** Tank them apart. Lord Kri cleaves and leaves poison; Princess Yauj fears and heals; Vem charges and enrages the others when he dies.

### 3. Battleguard Sartura
The "Whirlwind" fight. Requires heavy crowd control on adds.
* **Adds:** Focus down the three Royal Guards first. They can be Stunned, Feared, and Interrupted.
* **Whirlwind:** Sartura spends much of the fight in Whirlwind, dropping threat and moving randomly. Ranged must spread out; Melee must run out when she spins.

### 4. Fankriss the Unyielding
A pure DPS check with a tank swap mechanic.
* **Mortal Wound:** Reduces healing by 10%. Tanks must swap at 3–5 stacks.
* **Spawn of Fankriss:** Large worms spawn. They **must** be killed within 20 seconds or they enrage and one-shot players.
* **Vekniss Drones:** Trash adds spawn constantly; have Off-Tanks or Paladins pick them up for AoE.

### 5. Viscidus
Optional but highly technical. Requires **Frost Damage** to freeze.
* **Freeze Mechanic:** Hit him with ~200 Frost attacks (Frostbolts, Frost Wand, or melee with Frost Oil). 
* **Shatter:** Once frozen, hit him with ~30 Physical attacks to shatter him into globs.
* **The Globs:** Kill as many globs as possible before they reach the center. The boss's health only drops when globs die.

### 6. Princess Huhuran
The "Nature Resist" Wall.
* **The Frenzy:** At 30% HP, she enters a Berserk state, firing poison bolts at the 15 closest players.
* **Soak Rotation:** The 15 players with the highest **Nature Resistance** (usually Melee) must stay closest to her to "soak" the damage while the rest of the raid burns her down.

---

### 🔥 The Final Guardians

#### 7. The Twin Emperors
A split-damage fight between a Physical-immune boss and a Magic-immune boss.
* **Vek'nilash (Warrior):** Only takes Physical damage. Needs a Warrior/Druid tank.
* **Vek'lor (Warlock):** Only takes Magic damage. Needs a **Warlock Tank** with high Shadow Resistance.
* **Teleport:** They swap positions every 30 seconds. Tanks must be ready to grab the opposite twin instantly.

#### 8. C'Thun
The Old God. A two-phase fight where one mistake can chain-react and wipe the raid.

| Phase | Mechanic | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **P1: Eye** | *Eye Beam* | Chains to nearby players, doubling damage each jump. **STAY SPREAD.** |
| | *Dark Glare* | A slow-moving 180-degree red beam. If it touches you, you die. Rotate with it. |
| **P2: Body** | *Stomach* | Random players are swallowed. Kill the two tentacles inside to weaken C'Thun. |
| | *Vulnerability* | When the stomach tentacles die, C'Thun becomes vulnerable for 45s. **Pop all CDs.** |

> [!DANGER]
> **Spacing:** In P1, if two people stand within 10 yards of each other, the Eye Beam will likely kill at least one of them. Use a "Strict Spread" formation.

### 💎 Notable Loot
* **Tier 2.5 Tokens:** Dropped by Twin Emps, Ouro, and C'Thun.
* **C'Thun's Eye:** Starts a quest for a powerful necklace or caster cloak.
* **Dark Edge of Insanity:** Iconic 2H Axe with a disorient proc.
